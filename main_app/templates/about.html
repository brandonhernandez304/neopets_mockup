About Neopets

Hello and welcome! Neopets is a small mock-up of the original Neopets site, where their users can own a neopet and feed them, play with them, accessorize them, play games together and more!

Feel free to make an account so you can get started with adopting your neopets, feeding them meals, and giving them toys so they can be entertained.

There are many neopets to be collected, so in the near future users can expect the addition of a third party API containing all possible neopets so that you can adopt them with their preloaded images!

Thanks for stopping by! Feel free to check out the real site at neopets.com
